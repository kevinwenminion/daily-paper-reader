## 摘要
采用机器学习势驱动的分子动力学模拟（ML-MD）从原子层面揭示了本征和掺杂MgH2的脱氢动力学。通过对不同表面取向的系统研究，确定了MgH2 (100)表面是最活跃的氢释放低指数表面。对于本征MgH2，我们的模拟揭示了一种新颖的H2形成机制，其特征是在亚表层区域生成H2，随后扩散到表面进行脱附，突显了亚表层过程在传统表面驱动路径之外的关键作用。对22种掺杂元素的全面筛选表明，Ni是最有效的掺杂剂。在多个描述符中，机器学习分析确定时间耦合的Miedema电子密度是关键描述符，强调了电子特性的作用。因此，在固有Miedema电子密度（nws）与总氢释放之间发现了火山型关系（最佳窗口：4.0 < nws < 5.4×10^-2 e/bohr^3）。在此范围内的掺杂剂具有双重功能：作为吸引H的热力学汇，同时保持平衡的相互作用强度以促进H-H耦合和H2释放。这种原子层面的验证为实验观察到的催化相“氢泵”效应提供了强有力的理论支持。本研究展示了ML-MD在探索复杂催化机制和建立定量性质-活性关系方面的强大能力，为合理设计MgH2及其他储氢材料的高性能催化剂提供了稳健框架。

## Abstract
Machine learning potential-driven molecular dynamics simulations (ML-MD) were employed to provide atomistic insights into the dehydrogenation kinetics of pristine and doped MgH2. Through systematic investigation of distinct surface orientations, the MgH2 (100) surface was identified as the most active low-index surface for hydrogen release. For pristine MgH2, our simulations revealed a novel H2 formation mechanism characterized by H2 generation in the subsurface region followed by diffusion to the surface for desorption, highlighting the critical role of subsurface processes beyond conventional surface-driven pathways. Comprehensive screening of 22 doping elements identified Ni as the most effective dopant. Among several descriptors, machine learning analysis identified the time-coupled Miedema electron density as the critical descriptor, underscoring the role of electronic properties. Consequently, a volcano-shaped relationship was uncovered between the intrinsic Miedema electron density ( nws ) and total hydrogen release (optimal window: 4.0 < nws < 5.4x10-2 e/bohr3). Dopants within this range serve a dual function: acting as thermodynamic sinks for H attraction while maintaining a balanced interaction strength to facilitate H-H coupling and H2 release. This atomistic-level validation provides strong theoretical support for the experimentally observed "hydrogen pump" effect of catalytic phases. The present study demonstrates the strong capability of ML- MD in navigating through complex catalytic mechanisms and establishing quantitative property-activity relationships, providing a robust framework for rational design of high-performance catalysts for MgH2 and other hydrogen storage materials.

# 6. 论文的主要结论与发现

1. **表面活性排序**：MgH₂(100)表面脱氢活性最高，(001)、(101)、(110)依次降低。活性受表面开放结构与表面能共同影响。
2. **本征脱氢机制**：H₂并非在顶层表面形成，而是在亚表层（第二、三层H原子）通过H-H耦合生成，随后扩散至表面脱附。DFT计算支持亚表层H₂-H₃组合具有最低脱附能（1.59 eV）。
3. **最佳掺杂剂**：22种元素中Ni催化活性最高，DSC实验证实Ni掺杂使活化能从171 kJ/mol降低至135和101 kJ/mol（双峰）。
4. **关键描述符**：Miedema电子密度（\(n_{ws}\)）是决定催化活性的首要物理量，与氢释放量呈现火山型关系，最佳窗口为 \(4.0 < n_{ws} < 5.4 \times 10^{-2} \, \text{e/bohr}^3\)。
5. **催化机理**：Ni在MD过程中自发形成类似Mg₂NiH₄的局域团簇（Ni-Mg配位数7-8，Ni-H配位数4-5），充当“氢泵”：吸引周围H原子，促进H-H耦合生成H₂，并协助H₂释放。
6. **Sabatier原则验证**：掺杂剂需具备适中的H吸附强度——太弱（如La、Zr）无法有效吸引H，太强（如W）则阻碍H₂脱附。
