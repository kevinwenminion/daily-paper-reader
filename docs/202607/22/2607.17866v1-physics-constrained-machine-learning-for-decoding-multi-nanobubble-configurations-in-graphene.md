## 摘要
从电子光谱中识别多个石墨烯纳米气泡具有挑战性，因为它们的应变诱导特征重叠。我们开发了一个物理约束的机器学习框架，从态密度（DOS）光谱中解码纳米气泡构型。对于空间分离的纳米气泡，先前的全量子输运计算已证实，多气泡的DOS数值上等于组成它的单气泡光谱的归一化和。我们将这一验证过的加性关系编码到一个紧凑的神经分解模型中。对于每个目标光谱，基系数独立优化，得到的权重直接识别出组成几何结构。该方法能准确重构复杂度递增的构型，并且对重复成分、不完整的基字典以及模拟测量噪声保持稳健。该框架为表征应变工程石墨烯纳米结构提供了一条可解释的途径，并可能扩展到其他具有加性光谱响应的量子材料。

## Abstract
Identifying multiple graphene nanobubbles from electronic spectra is challenging because their strain-induced features overlap. We develop a physics-constrained machine-learning framework that decodes nanobubble configurations from density-of-states (DOS) spectra. For spatially separated nanobubbles, previous full quantum-transport calculations established that the multi-bubble DOS is numerically equivalent to the normalized sum of the constituent single-bubble spectra. We encode this validated additive relation in a compact neural decomposition model. For each target spectrum, the basis coefficients are optimized independently, and the resulting weights directly identify the constituent geometries. The method accurately reconstructs configurations of increasing complexity and remains robust to repeated constituents, incomplete basis dictionaries, and simulated measurement noise. The framework provides an interpretable route for characterizing strain-engineered graphene nanostructures and may extend to other quantum materials with additive spectral responses.
